Pineapple Bun

The Hong Kong-style pineapple bun is a soft, sweet bread topped with a golden, crumbly crust that resembles a pineapple—though it contains no actual pineapple. Often enjoyed warm and sometimes served with a slice of butter, it’s a popular treat in Hong Kong bakeries and tea houses.
