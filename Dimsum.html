Dimsum

Hong Kong-style dim sum is a traditional Cantonese cuisine featuring a variety of small, flavorful dishes served in steamer baskets or on small plates. Popular items include shu mai, har gow, spring rolls, and custard buns. Often enjoyed during brunch with tea, dim sum is known for its delicate flavors, bite-sized portions, and vibrant presentation.
