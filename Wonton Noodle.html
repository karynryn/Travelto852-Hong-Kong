Wonton Noodle

Hong Kong-style wonton noodles feature delicate shrimp or pork-filled wontons served in a clear, flavorful broth with thin, springy egg noodles. Often garnished with leafy greens and green onions, this comforting dish is a beloved staple of Hong Kong cuisine, known for its light yet savory taste and satisfying texture.
